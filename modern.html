Introduction

Modern computers are powerful machines used for business, research, gaming, and artificial intelligence. They are faster, smaller, and more energy-efficient than earlier generations.

Specifications

  • High-speed multi-core processors (Intel i9, AMD Ryzen 9, Apple M2)
  • Large RAM capacities (16GB – 128GB)
  • Fast storage (SSD, NVMe)
  • Dedicated GPUs for graphics-intensive applications
  • Advanced AI and machine learning capabilities
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5, Thunderbolt ports

Fun Fact

Modern supercomputers can perform over a quintillion calculations per second!

Notable Modern Computers

  • Apple MacBook Pro M2
  • Dell XPS Series
  • HP Z Workstations
  • IBM Summit Supercomputer
  • Cray EX Supercomputers
